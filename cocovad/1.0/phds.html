Ph.Ds Hiring

Page under construction! Stay tuned here or contact J.-M. Bruel.

The CoCoVaD Chair will hire three Ph.Ds in 2022-2023. The goal of these Ph.Ds, appart from addressing the key issues of the project, is to prepare high-level junior engineers to join the excellence tracks provided at Airbus for high potential and experts.

M.Sc. hiring

As part of the recruitement of talented Ph.D. candidates, we offer a first M.Sc. Internship for final Engineering Schools or Masters students willing to spend their final year Internship in a promising research environment. Details upcoming…​
